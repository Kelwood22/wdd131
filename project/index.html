How We Make Our Macarons

At Sweet Starlight Bakery, crafting the perfect macaron is a labor of love and precision. Every step of the process is approached with care, starting with sifting the finest almond flour and whipping meringue to a glossy perfection. The shells are baked to achieve their signature crisp exterior and chewy center. Each macaron is then filled with luscious creams, velvety ganaches, or seasonal jams, all made in-house. It's an art form where passion meets flavor, creating bites of pure enchantment.
